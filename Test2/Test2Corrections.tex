\section*{1.A}
$$ \vec{P} = [x(t)]\, \ihat + [y(t)], \jhat $$
$$ \vec{P} = [\int x'(t)dt] \, \ihat + [\int y'(t)dt +y_0] \, \jhat $$
$$ \vec{P} = [\frac{1}{(t+1)^2} +C_x] \, \ihat + [\tanh(t)+C_y] \, \jhat $$

Solve $C_x$
$$\frac{1}{(0+1)^2} +C_x = 1$$
$$1 +C_x = 1$$
$$C_x = 0$$

Solve $C_y$
$$\tanh(0) +C_y = 0$$
$$0 +C_y = 0$$
$$C_y = 0$$

Final vector is 
$$ \vec{P} = [\frac{1}{(t+1)^2}] \, \ihat + [\tanh(t)] \, \jhat $$
